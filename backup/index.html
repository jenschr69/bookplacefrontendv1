Frequently asked questions

Is the service really 100% free to use?

Yes, of course!

How many active users are there?

Around 100 000 each months.

Who owns the rights to all of the reviews posted?

You are, but bookplace can use them as long as they are active in our system. But you are of course free to reuse your own reviews where ever you want. And you can of course remove/delete your reviews at any time.

Why are some books missing a score?

Some books are missing a score because they simply haven't been reviewed yet.

Why can't I find a specific book?

This is because the book haven't been added yet by bookplace or one of our users.

What do I do when I find an error?

If you find an error on our website, we would really appreciate if you could let us know. Please send a mail to error@bookplace.com, and we will take a look at it as soon as possible.
